Chapter 3

Courage

Without a second thought,
Robin entered Sam's name into Google
and found her Instagram.
Robin nearly jumped out of her chair.
She couldn't believe she finally found Sam.
Some of her posts were her photography, others selfies, but most were photos with her friends. She felt more discouraged the further she scrolled down the page. Sam seemed really happy, and Robin was glad she was, but she couldn’t help feeling a little sad that Sam might’ve forgotten about her. A million thoughts rushed through her mind:

What if this was a mistake and she didn’t want to hear from me? Or worse, what if she didn’t remember our promise? What if I was just interrupting her amazing life with her amazing friends? What if, what if, what if...

Robin pushed back her chair and stepped away from her desk. As she paced back and forth, debating whether or not she should message Sam, small bits of yellow, blues, pinks and other colours caught her eye.
